The race to Mars has heated up again. With new propulsion technologies and international cooperation, the dream of setting foot on another planet is closer than ever.

## Challenges Ahead

The journey to Mars is fraught with peril. Radiation, isolation, and the sheer distance make it the most difficult engineering challenge humanity has ever faced.

> "Mars is not just a destination; it's a test of our resilience as a species." - Mission Director

### Timeline
- **2025**: Lunar Gateway operational
- **2028**: Sample return mission
- **2035**: First crewed landing
