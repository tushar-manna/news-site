Concrete jungles are slowly turning green. From Milan to Singapore, vertical forests are redefining the skyline and the way we live in cities.

## Benefits of Biophilic Design

Integrating plants into building structures does more than just look good. It helps regulate temperature, filter air pollutants, and reduce noise.

*   **Air Quality**: Plants absorb CO2 and release oxygen.
*   **Biodiversity**: Creating habitats for birds and insects.
*   **Well-being**: Connection to nature reduces stress.
